About Kettlebell Volume Calculator

Kettlebell Volume Calculator is a free, no-fluff tool designed to help kettlebell enthusiasts, trainers, and fitness beginners track the total workload and estimated calorie burn of their workouts. Whether you're doing EMOMs, Tabatas, or your own custom circuit, this tool gives you quick insight into how much you're really lifting in each session.

It was built by a developer who’s also passionate about strength training, conditioning, and making useful fitness tools that are easy to access and even easier to use — no signups, no downloads, just results.

If this tool helps you hit your goals or plan smarter workouts, that’s a win.
