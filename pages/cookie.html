What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit.

How We Use Cookies

Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect how this website functions.
